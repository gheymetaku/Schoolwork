  1. Unadon is a dish orignating from Japan
  2. It consists of a bowl filled with steaming hot white rice topped with fillets of eel
  3. The unagi fillets are glazed in a sweet sauce and caramelized over a charcoal fire
  4. Unadon was the first donburi type dish of its kind
  5. It was invented by a man named Imasuke Okubo around 1804
